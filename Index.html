Mission

Pie’s mission is to empower small businesses by making commercial insurance as easy as pie— modern, affordable, and human. Front End Engineers craft the experience that customers, agents, and partners rely on every day.

Why this role?

  • Own experiences end‑to‑end: from design reviews to production reliability.
  • Partner with design, product, and backend teams in a transparent, collaborative process.
  • Shape our engineering practices and FE architecture as a senior voice on the team.

Impact

  • Ship features that directly move company KPIs and customer outcomes.
  • Elevate front‑end delivery pipelines and developer experience.
  • Mentor other engineers; set a high bar for quality and a11y.

Role Highlights

Architecture & Ownership

  • Define and evolve FE architecture (SSR/SSG, micro‑frontends, module federation).
  • Design for performance, security, and maintainability.
  • Participate in on‑call/incident response; improve stability and visibility.

Product Delivery

  • Lead scoping with PM/Design; assess feasibility and shape requirements.
  • Implement accessible, responsive UIs with React + TypeScript.
  • Drive testing strategy (unit, integration, e2e) in CI/CD pipelines.

Quality & Collaboration

  • Champion patterns, testing, and docs; mentor through PRs and pairing.
  • Help teams adopt micro‑FE best practices and performance budgets.
  • Partner closely with backend to deliver clean contracts and resilient UX.
